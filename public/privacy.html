PRIVACY POLICY

Last updated: May 18, 2026

The Great Divide ("we," "us") is a free quiz at thegreatdivide.xyz that helps you measure your political bias. We keep things minimal — here's exactly what we collect and why.

What we collect

  • Anonymous gameplay analytics via Google Analytics 4: page views, button clicks, quiz completions, your final bias score and archetype, and aggregated answer patterns. No personal identifiers.
  • Contact form submissions: if you write to us through the Contact button, we store your name, email, and message for up to 30 days so we can reply.
  • Debate Mode room data: when you play live with friends, your chosen display name and answers are stored for up to 2 hours to run the game, then automatically deleted.
  • Error reports via Sentry: if the site crashes, technical details (browser, URL, stack trace) are sent so we can fix it. No personal info is included.

What we don't collect

  • No accounts, no passwords, no profiles.
  • No IP addresses stored beyond standard server logs.
  • No selling, no sharing with third parties for advertising.
  • No tracking pixels from social networks.

Cookies

Google Analytics sets standard cookies (_ga, _ga_*) to count unique visitors. You can block them via your browser settings or extensions like uBlock Origin without breaking the quiz.

Donations

Donations are processed by Ko-fi, which has its own privacy policy. We never see your payment details — we only receive the amount.

Your rights

You can ask us to delete any data we have about you (basically: contact form submissions) by emailing playthegreatdivide@gmail.com. We respond within 7 days.

Children

This site is not directed at children under 13. If you are under 13, please don't use the contact form.

Changes

If we change this policy, we'll update the date at the top. Material changes will be announced on the site.

Contact

Questions? playthegreatdivide@gmail.com
